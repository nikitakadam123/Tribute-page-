Career of Google CEO Sundar Pichai
  • He finished tutoring in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, a Central Board of Secondary Education school in Ashok Nagar, Chennai.
  • He finished the Class XII from Vana Vani school at IIT Madras.
  • He procured his certificate from IIT Kharagpur in metallurgical designing.
  • The, he went to United States.
  • He procured M.S. degree from Stanford University in materials science and designing
  • Then, at that point, he achieved a MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, where he was named a Siebel Scholar and a Palmer Scholar.
  • Pichai began his vocation as a materials engineer.
  • n 2004, he joined Google and turned into its CEO in 11 years
 
 
Google Journey

Starting his career from McKinsey &Co after completing his Wharton MBA, Sundar Pichai grabbed the golden opportunity to work at Google in the year 2004. Pichai’s Google venture started as the President of Product Management, he was initially asked to enhance the user experience of the Google search toolbar which could be accessed through Firefox and Internet Explorer. Further, he was also involved with a variety of Google products like Google Pack, Gadgets, Google Gears, and many more.

Later on came the breakthrough in Sundar’s Google journey, when he started working closely for Google’s very own browser, Chrome. The year 2008 came out to be marvelous with the launch of Google Chrome and the announcement of Pichai being the Vice President of Product Management and Development. By the end of the year 2012, he was promoted as a Senior Vice President followed by Product Chief Officer for Google as well as the Android Smartphone Operating System.
